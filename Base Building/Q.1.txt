The Internet is a massive, interconnected network of computers and devices that spans the entire globe. It allows these devices to communicate with each other, sharing information and enabling various online services. Think of it as a vast digital landscape where people, businesses, and organizations can connect, exchange data, and access a multitude of resources.

Through the Internet, individuals can browse websites, send emails, participate in social media, and engage in countless online activities. It operates on a system of protocols that ensure standardized communication, facilitating the seamless exchange of information across different devices and platforms.
